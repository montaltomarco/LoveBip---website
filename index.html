Presentation

We want to create a new brand of elegant and fashion wearables. We named LoveBip our first product. LoveBip is a a new way to transfer touch and emotions between two people. Created with fashion appeal in mind, LoveBip bracelets connect you and your beloved one as if you never left each other. It is a way to say the most important thing to the most important person.

Your beloved is the only one who will feel that you touched your bracelet. No matter how far away you are from each other, it feels like you just touched his hand. Two LoveBip bracelets are connected only to each other, same as you two are.

LoveBip also connects to a mobile application using Bluetooth technology and sends alerts to you when you receive an important message or call on your smartphone.

  • LoveBip mobile application

    You can simply download the mobile app and connect to the bracelet through Bluetooth. The app will then ask you if you want to pair your bracelet with the other bracelet.

    Available for iOS platform, the application is customizable to allow you to choose which emotions will be sent to your love or to simply add a new emotion.

    You can also select the most important notifications — calls, messages or apps you want to come through to your bracelets. Choose from 5 vibration patterns and 3 light colors to create a unique communication code with your beloved and customize the notifications.

Pre-incubation at FEE LyonTech

The start-up project joins the Entrepreneurship Program within INSA Lyon. We follow a pedagogical acceleration curriculum, to build the value proposition, the prototype, the business model and the financing strategy.
